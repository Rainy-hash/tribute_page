Film Appearances:
  • 2006, Lego Batman had his first film appearance in a short animated film on Cartoon Network "Lego Batman: Bricks, Bats & Bad Guys"
  • 2014, Lego Batman appeared in The Lego Movie
  • 2017, Lego Batman had his own film, The Lego Batman Movie, it's kind of like the original Lego Movie only vastly superior since it revolves around him
  • 2019, Lego Batman has a role in Lego Movie 2:The Second Part
    • Video Game Appearances:
  • 2008, Lego Batman gets his first video game, Lego Batman The Video Game
  • 2012, the sequel to Lego Batman The Video Game, Lego Batman 2: DC Super Heroes makes its debut
  • 2014, Lego Batman 3: Beyond Gotham releases, a massive upgrade to his former games
